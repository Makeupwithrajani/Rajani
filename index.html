Common Questions

FAQs

Do you offer on-location services?

+

Yes! I travel to your home, hotel, or event venue across Kathmandu Valley. For destinations outside Kathmandu, travel fees may apply. Just mention your location when booking.

How far in advance should I book for bridal makeup?

+

I recommend booking bridal appointments as early as possible, especially during wedding season (October–December and February–May). Popular dates fill up quickly, so reaching out 2–6 months in advance is ideal.

Do you do a trial session before the wedding?

+

Yes, a trial session is highly recommended for brides and is available as an add-on. The trial lets us perfect your look, test products on your skin, and make sure you're 100% happy before the big day.

What products do you use? Are they safe for sensitive skin?

+

I use professional-grade, hygienic products. If you have sensitive skin, allergies, or any skin conditions, please mention it during consultation so I can choose the safest products for you.

Can you do makeup for the whole bridal party?

+

Absolutely! I can take care of the bride, bridesmaids, mothers, and family members. For larger groups, we'll create a schedule to ensure everyone is ready on time. Get in touch for group rates.

How do I book and confirm an appointment?

+

The easiest way is to message on WhatsApp at +977 9840141090. You can also DM on Instagram or fill out the contact form. Once we discuss your needs, I'll confirm your appointment and share all details.
