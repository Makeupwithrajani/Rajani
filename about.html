Hi, I'm Rajani 👋

Rajani Shrestha

I'm a professional Makeup & Hair Stylist proudly based in Kathmandu, Nepal. Makeup is not just my career — it's my passion and a way for me to express creativity while bringing joy to others.

My passion for makeup began as a hobby and soon transformed into a full-time profession. Today, I help clients feel confident and beautiful with bridal looks, party glam, photoshoot makeup, and professional hair styling — always tailored to each person's unique style and features.

Whether it's a bride on her most important day, a photoshoot for a brand or personal project, or a party look that needs to turn heads — I love collaborating with clients to create results that leave us both feeling proud.

My goal is simple: to make everyone in my chair feel valued, cared for, and utterly confident. I can't wait to hear from you and hopefully work together soon!

The Approach

Every Client
Deserves the Best

I believe that every face is unique — and every look should be too. Before any appointment, I take time to understand your skin, your vision, your outfit, and the mood of the occasion.

From the first conversation to the final touch-up, I am with you every step of the way. My approach is calm, professional, and always centered on making you feel completely amazing.

I focus on clean, long-lasting finishes using professional-grade, hygienic products — so you look flawless and feel comfortable throughout your entire event.

The Values

What Drives
My Work

💛

Passion Over Profession

Makeup was never just a job for me — it started as a creative outlet and grew into a calling. That passion shows in every look I create, no matter how big or small the occasion.

🤝

Collaboration, Not Imposition

I never force a look onto someone. My process is collaborative — I listen, I understand, and I create. Your vision, your face, your personality — all at the center of everything.

✦

Attention to Detail

The difference between good and great is in the details. From the precise arch of a brow to the seamless blend of a contour, every small decision matters in the final look.

🌸

Confidence Through Beauty

The most rewarding moment is when a client looks in the mirror and their face lights up. That feeling — of seeing yourself at your most beautiful — is what I work toward every single time.
